The 2016-2017 Model UN Executive Board

President: Josie Del Rio

The President is the presiding officer at all meetings. The president is responsible for creating an agenda for the general club meeting as well as the Executive Board meetings. Additionally, the president is responsible for representing the club in relation to the University of Pittsburgh as a whole. The president may hold no other offices in the club.

Head Delegate: Tyler Duddy

The Head Delegate is in charge of training new members. The Head Delegate also plans and runs our in-house training conferences. In addition to registering for conferences, the Head Delegate represents the team at the Head Delegate meetings while at conferences. The goal of the Head Delegate is to provide the necessary support for the team to be successful at conferences.

Secretary General: Priya Chandrasekaran

The Secretary General of the high school conference is responsible for working with the University Center for International Studies (UCIS) in planning for and executing the University of Pittsburgh High School Model United Nations Conference. The Secretary General may choose Under Secretaries, but these individuals are not club officers.

Director of Finance: Rebecca Kerman

The Director of Finance is responsible for preparing the annual budget and supplemental budget requests. Additionally, the Director of Finance is responsible for process all club business involving these budget items, such as business travel advances and disbursements. The Director of Finance is responsible for attending appropriate budget hearings related to club finances.

Director of External Affairs: Jon McMullin

The Director of External Affairs is responsible for planning the recruitment of new members. In addition, the Director of External Affairs communicates with the Model UN teams of nearby schools to coordinate events. The Director of External Affairs is also in charge of communicating with organizations within and the University of Pittsburgh and the outside community.
