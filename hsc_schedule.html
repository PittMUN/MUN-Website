  Tentative Schedule

All sessions will be in the William Pitt Union and O'Hara Student Center
All events on the schedule will be occuring in the William Pitt Union (location) unless otherwise specified.

8:30 a.m.  

Registration

9:00 a.m.  

Introduction/Announcements

9:30 a.m.  

Committee Meetings
Those students whose committees are in the O'Hara Student Center will be escorted there at this time.

11:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m.  

Staggered lunch schedule
Lunches will occur in the same building as students' committee sessions.

1:30 to 4:30 p.m.  

Committee meetings continue

4:30 p.m.  

General Assembly/Closing Ceremonies
Students in the O'Hara student center will be escorted back to the William Pitt Union at this time.

5:00 p.m.  

Dismissal

PLEASE NOTE THAT ALL PARTICIPANTS ARE EXPECTED TO STAY FOR THE ENTIRE DAY.

If your school cannot do this, please do not register.
