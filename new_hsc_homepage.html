THE DATE FOR THE 2016 PITTMUN HIGH SCHOOL CONFERENCE IS COMING SOON

Model UN is a simulation of the sessions of the United Nations. This is an opportunity for students to apply prior research and studies in a real-world context and practice diplomacy, negotiating, and resolution writing. Since 1996 The University of Pittsburgh's University Center for International Studies along with Pitt's own Model UN Club has provided a conference for students to show their strengths, and compete against one another in a one day Model UN simulation.

The 20th annual PittMUN High School Conference is just around the corner, so to help get you started, we've provided some links to helpful information below. To see everything compiled in one list, please click here, or on the "General Info" button below.

If you have any questions about the High School Conference, please don't hesistate to contact the club at PittModelUN@gmail.com, including "High School Conference" in the subject line.
We look forward to seeing you in November!
