  Resources Available through University of Pittsburgh
  ABCs of the U.N.
This pamphlet offers a short history, structure, purposes, principles, and membership of the U.N. It also highlights some of the subsidiary organs, specialized agencies, and organizations created by or related to the U.N. Published by the United Nations Association.
  United Nations Curriculum Guide
The curriculum guide is a collection of activities and materials on the United Nations designed for high school students. The book was the result of a collaboration between the United Nations Association of America, the Association of Teacher Educators and the Stanley Foundation.

  The United Nations at a Glance
A fact sheet detailing the committees, agencies, members, and other information relating to the General Assembly, Security Council, Economic and Social Council, Trusteeship Council, International Court of Justice, and the Secretariat. Published by the United Nations Association.
