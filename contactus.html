High School Conference

For any questions regarding the High School Conference, please contact the Pitt Model UN group by email at: PittModelUN@gmail.com

Be sure to include PittMUN High School Conference in the subject line.

Please note that during the University of Pittsburgh's Summer Break (May 1-August 20th) it may take slightly longer for us to return your emails. We will do our best to respond as quickly as possible, and we apologize for any inconvenience.

General Club Questions

All general club questions can be directed to PittModelUN@gmail.com

For questions about a specific Executive Board duty, feel free to reach out to that club member directly.

For more information on the 2016-2017 club Board, please see the Club Members page.
